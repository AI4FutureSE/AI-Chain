Task Modeling

Promptmanship: process, concepts, activities, and patterns

During exploration, task modeling utilizes magic-enhancing- magic to gain an understanding of task-related knowledge, obtain preliminary task requirements, workflow, input/output data and constraints, and initially align task decomposition with model capabilities, while also exploring different prompts to gauge their effectiveness.

For example, when building SiXiaoPin (a mock interview service), the engineer learned from ChatGPT the general workflow of an interview, the information to be collected from the interviewee at each step, and different aspects assessed during interviews. He also experimented prompts for generating interview questions and found that the LLM sometimes generates questions in form of suggestions such as ``You may ask: ... an interview question ...'', but not as an interviewer. He found that this issue can be addressed by setting up persona and output formatter.

Such an initial task model provides the foundation for system design and will be continuously elaborated during the AI chain design and construction process, and further evolved driven by the end-users' feedback after deployment.
